   ## Loan Management App: Detailed Functionality

**User Roles:**

* Borrower: Individuals managing their personal loans.
* Loan Officer: Individuals managing loans for a financial institution.
* System Administrator (Optional): Manages user accounts and system settings (for larger institutions).

**Core Functionalities:**

1. **Loan Management:**
    * **Borrower:**
        * View a list of all their active loans.
        * Access detailed information for each loan (amount, interest rate, term, repayment schedule, remaining balance).
        * Make loan payments (manual entry or integration with bank accounts).
        * Download payment history or loan statements.

    * **Loan Officer:**
        * Create new loan applications.
        * Edit existing loan details (excluding core borrower information).
        * Manage loan documents (upload, download, and share securely)..
        * View borrower details and creditworthiness information (with proper authorization).

    * **System Administrator (Optional):**
        * Manage user accounts (create, edit, delete) and assign roles.
        * Configure system settings (interest rate calculations, fee structures).



3. **Reporting and Analytics:**
    * **Borrower Reports:**
        * Track overall loan performance and remaining balances.
        * View payment history and identify any missed payments.
        * Export reports in various formats (PDF, CSV) for further analysis.

    * **Loan Officer Reports:**
        * Monitor loan portfolio performance and identify potential delinquencies.
        * Generate reports on loan types, interest rates, and borrower demographics.
